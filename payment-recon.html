Logic in this build

2. Amount match = Finance Total amount received vs SF Amount Paid
3. Scholarship match = Finance scholarship discount vs SF Outstanding Payment
4. Differences are shown as Finance - SF
5. Round-off bucket = difference above 0.01 and up to 1.00
